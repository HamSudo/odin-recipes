Description

Scrambled hot dogs and eggs is a quick and satisfying dish that combines sliced or chopped hot dogs with fluffy scrambled eggs. The hot dogs are usually pan-fried first, giving them a lightly browned, slightly crispy exterior and a smoky, savory flavor. Once the hot dogs are cooked, beaten eggs are poured over them and gently stirred until they set into soft curds, binding everything together. The resulting mix has a warm, golden color with pops of pink or brown from the hot dogs, making it visually simple but comforting.

This dish is often enjoyed as a hearty breakfast or an easy dinner, especially when time or ingredients are limited. It can be seasoned with just salt and pepper or jazzed up with extras like onions, cheese, or hot sauce for added flavor. Scrambled hot dogs and eggs are popular in many households because they’re economical, kid-friendly, and require minimal prep. Served with toast, rice, or wrapped in a tortilla, it’s a versatile and nostalgic meal for many.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 1 hot dog, cut into 1/2-inch slices
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a small nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add hot dogs and cook until browned on all sides, about 2 minutes. Turn heat to down to medium-low.
  2. Whisk eggs with salt and pepper until well blended and pour over hot dogs. Cook, stirring constantly until eggs are set. Serve immediately.
