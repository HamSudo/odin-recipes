# Odin Recipes

Welcome to **Odin Recipes**! This website features a collection of easy-to-make and delicious recipes for a variety of meals. Whether you're looking for a quick breakfast, a hearty salad, or a flavorful Tex-Mex dish, you'll find something here.

## Recipes Included

- **[Cobb Salad](recipes/cobb-salad.html)**
  - A classic American salad with a colorful presentation.
  - Ingredients include bacon, chicken, hard-boiled eggs, avocado, tomatoes, blue cheese, and more.
  - Traditionally served with red wine vinaigrette or ranch dressing.
  
- **[Taco Salad III](recipes/taco-salad.html)**
  - A hearty Tex-Mex salad perfect for sharing.
  - Includes seasoned ground beef, beans, lettuce, avocado, tomatoes, cheese, corn chips, and Catalina dressing.
  - Easy to prepare in about 30 minutes and serves up to eight people.

- **[Scrambled Hot Dogs and Eggs](recipes/hot-dogs-and-eggs.html)**
  - A quick and satisfying breakfast or dinner option.
  - Combines sliced hot dogs with fluffy scrambled eggs.
  - Simple seasoning with salt and pepper, optional add-ons like cheese or onions.

## Features

- Simple, clean HTML structure.
- Recipe pages include:
  - **Description** – Learn what makes the dish special.
  - **Ingredients** – Full list of ingredients with quantities.
  - **Steps** – Easy-to-follow preparation instructions.
- Images for visual guidance.

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Click on a recipe link to view detailed instructions and ingredients.
3. Follow the steps to make your delicious meal!
